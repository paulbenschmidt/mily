# Vision

## Mission
1. To help people understand themselves, and life, better by encouraging self-reflection through a visually engaging timeline of their life events and opportunities to reflect on them.
2. To deepen relationships with others by allowing users to share their timelines with friends and family, and to connect with others who share similar life events.

## Platform
Mily is a personal timeline app that helps users record and reflect on their life events.

## Core Features
1. Timeline Creation: Users can record Major, Minor, and Other life events; add notes and photos to each event.
2. Timeline Sharing: Users can share their timelines with friends and family, and can set privacy levels for each shared timeline.

## Customer Experience
- The app should be minimalistic, with a focus on ease-of-use and the content of the app.


# Implementation

## Tech Stack

Frontend: Next.js 15 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
Backend: Django REST Framework + PostgreSQL
Database: Neon
Hosting: Vercel (frontend and backend)
