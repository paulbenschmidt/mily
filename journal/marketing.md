### 90-Day Roadmap
- Months 1-2:
    - [x] Update LinkedIn (30 min, one-time)
    - [ ] Find and deeply engage 15 users (primary focus)
        - [ ] Look at people who have reviewed previous apps or are active on mental health forums
        - [ ] Older, reflective generation
        - [ ] People in therapy (or therapists) or find blogs/websites that discuss mental health/therapy and reach out
        - [ ] Gather testimonials from users
        - [ ] Create Discord channel?
    - [ ] Participate helpfully in 2-3 Reddit communities (30 min/week)
    - [ ] Document every user conversation and testimonial
    - [ ] Dayton newspaper: Advertise it as a reflection tool, have them interview me
    - [ ] Entrepreneur's Center: Have them feature me in their newsletter
- Month 3:
    - [ ] Approach 5 partnerships with your case studies and user stories
    - [ ] Consider paid micro-targeted ads ($100-200 test budget) to communities like life coaches if organic isn't moving fast enough
- Months 4+:
    - [ ] Revisit content strategy only if you have consistent user stories to share
    - [ ] Consider Substack/Medium newsletter to existing users, not as acquisition

The hard truth: At this stage, nothing beats talking to humans one-on-one. Every hour you spend writing blog posts that no one reads is an hour you didn't spend finding user #7 or #8. Get to 15 users who genuinely love this, and your path forward will become much clearer - they'll tell you where to find more people like them.
